Overview

Established in 1970, VaksonGroupis a family-owned, diversified business group with deep roots in the UAE and a legacy of over 50 years. Headquartered in Dubai, we are a trusted name in real estate and beyond. Offering comprehensive services in real estate development, property management, facility maintenance, construction, brokerage, financial investments, valuations and consultancy.

Vision

To be the region’s most respected and sought-after real estate partner, known for our unwavering integrity, visionary leadership and meaningful contributions to national development.

Mission

To deliver strategic, high-quality real estate solutions that generate lasting value for investors, stakeholders and communities, while elevating the everyday experiences of our customers.

Core Values

Simplicity

Clear, efficient and straightforward processes.

Honesty

Transparent dealings with clients and partners.

Simplicity

Ethical standards in every transaction and decision.
