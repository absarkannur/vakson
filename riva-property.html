Riva Residences

Modern Waterfront Living Redefined

Experience the elegance of coastal living at Riva Residences, where contemporary design meets serene waterfront views. Nestled in a prime location, Riva offers spacious apartments with modern finishes, floor-to-ceiling windows, and private balconies overlooking the water. Enjoy premium amenities, including a state-of-the-art gym, infinity pool, and direct access to the marina promenade. Perfect for those seeking a lifestyle of sophistication and tranquility in the heart of the city.

  • Waterfront apartments with panoramic views
  • Luxury finishing & smart home features
  • Exclusive access to private beach and marina
  • Resort-style facilities & wellness spaces
